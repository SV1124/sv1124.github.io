About me

Fields of interest #

  • Active in finance law (mainly mid-market acquisition finance, representing both borrowers and lenders) and financial regulation (mainly funds law, but also crypto).

  • Broad interest in all things legal philosophy and legal history.

  • Interest in law governing church institutions.

  • I also keep up with data privacy and security, crypto, digital services, virtual assets and AI.

Professional experience #

  • Lawyer specialised in banking, financial and security interest law (2015-now). Until 2020 at Allen & Overy (Belgium) LLP (A&O Shearman Belgium), now at Argo Law.

  • Teaching assistant financial law at Jan Ronse Instituut KU Leuven (part time) (2023-now)

  • Voluntary teaching assistant commercial and insolveny law KU Leuven (2016-2019)

Education #

  • Law and technology program at KU Leuven (2024)

  • Managerial economics (bedrijfseconomie voor niet-economisten) at Antwerp Management School (2022)

  • Research master of Laws degree KU leuven (2010 - 2015), magna cum laude. Joint program with Tilburg University. Majored in tax & commercial law.

  • Exchange at New York University (2014) (focus on European Human Rights Law, US corporate and securities law, US fundamental tax cases. Attended classes a.o. on legal philosophy).
